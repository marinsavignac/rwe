Detailed Timeline

Purchase and Return:
I purchased a FREDERIQUE CONSTANT watch from RWE Imperial through eBay. I regretted my purchase and decided to return the item. With DHL, I arranged for the item to be returned directly to their listed address in Nagoya, Japan. DHL tracking records confirm that the return was delivered and signed for on November 14, 2024 by Ryuta Wanibe "Wany" himself.

Refund Policy & Response:
According to RWE Imperial’s stated policy, refunds are issued for unused products if contacted within two days of receipt. Despite my efforts to comply with these terms, RWE Imperial informed me that the eBay transaction superseded their policy and did not result in a refund.

eBay’s Role in the Dispute:
eBay initially facilitated communication, but the platform ruled against me, citing insufficient tracking evidence. This decision was based on the seller providing the original outbound tracking number rather than the DHL return tracking number, which I had submitted as proof of delivery.

Attempts to Resolve:
I contacted RWE Imperial directly through eBay and their official website. My reasonable requests for even a partial refund to account for potential shipping and transaction fees were ignored. The seller ceased collaboration altogether, leaving me without recourse.

Research into RWE Imperial:
My research indicated that other buyers have shared similar concerns about their experiences with RWE Imperial. Reviews and comments on platforms like eBay mention difficulties resolving refund disputes and dissatisfaction with customer service.

Why This Matters:
RWE Imperial’s handling of my case demonstrates a significant lack of accountability and customer service. By refusing to issue a refund despite undeniable evidence of return, they have failed to honor their own policies and undermined buyer trust. This experience has cost me financially and wasted countless hours attempting to seek resolution.

My Message to Future Buyers:
If you are considering a purchase from Ryuta Wanibe "Wany" Enterprise Imperial (RWE Imperial) or their shop, The Finest Luxury Shop Imperial, I urge you to proceed with caution. While their website and listings are well-presented, my experience suggests potential challenges that prospective buyers should consider.

Verify their refund policy: While their website lists clear terms, they may not honor them.
Beware of international purchases: If disputes arise, securing a resolution can be exceedingly difficult.
Document everything: Retain all communication and evidence, as it may be needed for legal action or public testimony.
